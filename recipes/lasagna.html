Lasagna

Ingredients

    For the Meat Sauce:

  • 500g (1 lb) ground beef
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 800g (28 oz) can crushed tomatoes
  • 170g (6 oz) can tomato paste
  • 60ml (1/4 cup) vegetable oil or melted butter
  • 400g (14 oz) can tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons sugar (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil leaves
  • 1 teaspoon dried Italian seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

    • For the Cheese Filling:

  • 450g (16 oz) ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

    • For Assembly:

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 450g (16 oz) shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Meat Sauce:

    2. In a large pot over medium heat, cook the ground beef, onion, and garlic until the meat is well browned. Drain any excess fat. Add the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, and water to the pot. Stir in the sugar (if using), basil, Italian seasoning, salt, pepper, and parsley. Simmer, uncovered, for about 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.

  2. Cook the Lasagna Noodles:

    3. While the sauce is simmering, bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook the lasagna noodles in the boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes, stirring occasionally until al dente. Drain and rinse with cold water. Set aside.

  3. Prepare the Cheese Filling:

    4. In a mixing bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, egg, Parmesan cheese, and parsley. Mix well.

  4. Assemble the Lasagna:

    5. Preheat your oven to 175°C (350°F). Spread 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Place 4 lasagna noodles lengthwise over the sauce. Spread one-third of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles. Top with one-third of the mozzarella cheese and 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce. Repeat the layers two more times, ending with the remaining mozzarella cheese and Parmesan cheese on top.

  5. Bake the Lasagna:

    6. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake it in the furnance 200º 20 min.
