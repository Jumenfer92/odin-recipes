Marinade Steak

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/4 cup balsamic vinegar/li>
  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar or honey
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Fresh herbs (such as rosemary, thyme, or parsley), chopped (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Marinade:

    2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, olive oil, Worcestershire sauce, balsamic vinegar, Dijon mustard, lemon juice, and brown sugar (or honey). Add the minced garlic, black pepper, onion powder, smoked paprika (if using), and red pepper flakes (if using). Whisk until all the ingredients are well combined. If desired, stir in fresh herbs for additional flavor.

  2. Marinate the Steak:

    3. Place your steak (about 4 medium steaks, or one large steak about 1 to 1.5 kg/2 to 3 lbs) in a large resealable plastic bag or a shallow dish. Pour the marinade over the steak, making sure it is well coated on all sides. Seal the bag or cover the dish, and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, preferably overnight for the best flavor. Turn the steak occasionally to ensure even marination.

  3. Prepare the Grill or Pan:

    4. When ready to cook, preheat your grill to high heat or heat a heavy skillet or grill pan over medium-high heat.

  4. Cook the Steak:

    5. Remove the steak from the marinade and shake off any excess liquid. Discard the marinade. Grill or pan-sear the steak to your desired level of doneness, using a meat thermometer to check the internal temperature (125°F for rare, 135°F for medium-rare, 145°F for medium, 155°F for medium-well, and 160°F for well-done).

  5. Rest the Steak:

    6. Once cooked, transfer the steak to a cutting board and let it rest for about 5 minutes before slicing. This allows the juices to redistribute throughout the meat, ensuring a juicy steak.

  6. Serve:

    7. Slice the steak against the grain and serve with your favorite sides, such as grilled vegetables, mashed potatoes, or a fresh salad.
